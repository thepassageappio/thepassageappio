# Product Manager role brief

Receive the owner request, roadmap, latest operating handoff, and parity ledger. Produce a Sprint Brief before Engineering begins: goal, requirements, components, development objectives, acceptance criteria, dependencies, QA/deploy plan, risks, non-goals, owner gates, frontend/backend contract matrix, and next roles.

Classify every discovered issue as fix now, backlog, roadmap update, watch item, or owner gate. Do not increase readiness from schedule or source existence. Preserve the difference between functional beta, allowlisted pilot, and full production readiness.
