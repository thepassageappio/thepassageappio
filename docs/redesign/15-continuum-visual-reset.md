# Continuum visual reset

Status: founder-directed replacement for the rejected Threshold-derived visual layer.

## Why the prior batch failed the greenfield bar

The workflow and role architecture changed, but the visible language remained recognizably Threshold:

- Fraunces/Georgia editorial display type
- bone, pine, and clay palette
- large serif statements
- rounded elevated cards
- similar mobile stacking and pill controls

That made a new information architecture read like another reskin. Continuum replaces the visible system rather than decorating the same composition.

## Product expression

**Quiet precision with human light.** Passage should feel calm enough for a family in crisis, operational enough for a funeral director, and current enough for millennial and Gen Z coordinators.

### Non-negotiable tokens

- Typography: system humanist sans only. No Fraunces, Georgia, or serif fallback.
- Canvas: cool porcelain, not cream.
- Text: graphite/navy, not pine.
- Primary signal: cobalt.
- Human warmth: restrained apricot.
- Shared/continuity state: luminous lilac and sky.
- Geometry: 6–14px radii, hairline rules, flat planes. Avoid pill/card repetition.
- Motion: continuity-line progress and restrained state transitions; reduced-motion equivalent required.
- Evidence IDs/times: monospace treatment.
- Accessibility: visible focus, semantic state, non-color labels, 48px mobile targets.

## Signature system

Every persona sees the same **continuity rail**:

`family intent → selected scope → receiving organization → accepted case → owned commitment → visible proof`

The rail changes density by role but not meaning. It is the visual and product-level representation of Passage's moat: a consented handoff with ownership and evidence.

## Persona compositions

### Family

A guided consent canvas, not a dashboard. The handoff object is the visual anchor. Recipient, scope, expiry, and what remains private stay visible as one connected decision.

### Director

A split-pane operating command surface. Risk, waiting, ownership, and the active handoff share one timeline. Avoid marketing-scale headings inside operational views.

### Employee

A precision work cockpit. One active commitment, a chronological rail, clear blockers, and proof destination. No organization-wide reporting chrome.

## Greenfield acceptance

- Still identifiable as Continuum with color removed.
- No serif display typography.
- No bone/pine/clay dependency.
- No page whose primary composition is a stack of rounded cards.
- Desktop and mobile use intentionally different arrangements.
- Family, director, and employee remain connected by the same continuity model but are not template clones.
