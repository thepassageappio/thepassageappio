Threshold

One system, two temperatures: warm and unhurried for grieving families, structured and dense-capable for the operators coordinating around them.

Color

Deep Pine for authority and continuity. Warm Clay for humanity and action. Bone paper underneath everything. No siren-red anywhere in the system — urgency is communicated through Clay, layout, and language, not alarm color.

Type

One rule at every decision: moment, or mechanism? Fraunces carries the moments — names, milestones, empathetic headlines. Inter carries the mechanisms — everything you act on, scan, or fill in.

Rule of thumb: if the sentence is being said to the family about their life, it's a moment (Fraunces). If it tells someone what to do or shows them data, it's a mechanism (Inter). A screen title can be Fraunces; the buttons and lists beneath it are always Inter.

Elevation, radius, motion

Elevation

Two levels only. Level 1 (resting cards, list rows) = barely-there. Level 2 (modals, active task drawer) = a soft lift, never a hard drop shadow. Nothing floats aggressively — this is a record, not a dashboard trying to impress.

Radius

10px standard, 6px for compact controls (pills, chips, inline badges). Consistent everywhere — no mixing of sharp admin corners and round consumer corners.

Motion

180–220ms ease-out on state changes. No bounce, no confetti, no celebratory motion tied to death-adjacent actions. Motion exists to orient ("this task just moved to done"), never to delight.

Components — tone in practice

Buttons

Primary = Pine (calm authority). Clay reserved for the one action that moves the family record forward. Never more than one Clay action per screen.

One kit, two temperatures

Same tokens, same components, same status language — density and pacing adapt to who's using it.

Family — warm

  • Mobile-first, one thing at a time
  • Generous whitespace, larger type
  • One clear next action per screen
  • Compassionate, plain-language copy
  • Progress always visible, never a raw percentage

Operator — structured

  • Desktop console, dense-capable tables
  • Funeral home / vendor / admin surfaces
  • Bulk actions, filters, exports
  • Zero ambiguity in ownership, always visible
  • Efficient over delightful — respects a busy desk
